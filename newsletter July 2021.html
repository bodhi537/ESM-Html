July 2021 Newsletter

Software Asset Management Release 2

Beginning in late August, 2500+ software products previously requested in BMC Remedy will be migrated and accessible via the new ServiceNow portal.

New features coming:

  • Enhanced integration and workflows
  • Better user experience and reduced cycle time
  • Improved control and better transparency
  • Enhanced capabilities such as approval delegation

Training sessions are planned for organizations providing operational support.

New IT Service Portal coming

Last month we shared some exciting features of the new IT Service Portal coming in late August. To minimize impacts, the portal is very similar to what exists today with some enhanced features.

Sign-up for training

Trainings are available for organizations onboarding to ServiceNow. For your convenience, multiple sessions will be offered between July and October. We have consolidated our fulfiller training sessions in an easy to access page.

eBonding improvements

Since inception we have eBonded (i.e. synchronized) >400k tickets between ServiceNow and BMC with a success rate of 80%. We have made improvements and beginning this month are experiencing 90% success. Given the level of accuracy and limited shelf life (~10 weeks), we will limit further enhancements but will best effort troubleshoot issues that arise.

Integration with TFS/ADO

Our custom integration between ServiceNow and Microsoft ADO/TFS will be available August 16th. Access this link to see the scope of this tactical integration solution, including what is different from today. Office hours will be scheduled and documentation provided soon to assist with setup.

A more robust integration is being evaluated for 2022 but will require a hard benefits to proceed.
