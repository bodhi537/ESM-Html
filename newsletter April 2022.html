Analytics and Reporting

New BMC historic metrics available in ServiceNow

High level ITDW BMC KPIs for historical trend purposes are now available in Service Now Analytics Hub. The monthly are for EMIT as a whole and include breakdowns as appropriate for the metric with trend data from Jan 2017 until BMC retirement. Any further analysis that may be required should use the BMC_Archive DB with a separate visualization tool such as Tableau. Search for ITDW in the Analytics Hub to see the new historic ITDW BMC KPIs.
