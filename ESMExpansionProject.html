2022 ESM Expansion Project




The 2020 Enterprise Service Management Project migrated EMIT from BMC to ServiceNow. This migration was completed as an end-of life initiative to remove BMC and associated costs from the environment. This project will execute opportunities to implement enhancements and strategic solutions to the ServiceNow offering such as HAM Pro and license optimization.Additionally, the project work with the base operations teams to categorize and execute work.


Key Deliverables and Achievements

July 2022:


  • Preparing for Gate 3

  • Completed MOC for ITIL and Business Stakeholder license management plans with ServiceNow Domain Users. Automations to be implemented before the end of the July

  • Progressed DBM/ServiceNow integration and expecting completions before the end of July


June 2022:


  • Planned DBM integration scope and provided a high level schedule for delivery with teams

  • Created ITIL and Business Stakeholder licensing plans to be communicated and executed within the next 2 months.

  • ServiceNow upgrade completed June 11th.


May 2022:


  • Passed Gate 2 with an emphasis on providing a solution for the ServiceNow DBM integrations for architect stakeholders (Preston H. to lead)

  • Updated ITIL licensing automation process reporting still underway. Expecting to have full report and full implementation plans before June 2022.

  • No major findings during the ServiceNow upgrade testing.


April 2022:


  • Finalized No Go decision for Hardware Asset Management Pro Proof of Value with CX, SD and CES Managers due to no hard benefits. CES will revisit HAM Pro decision with HCL directly to explore possibility of discounts as they gain capacity

  • ITIL license automation process successfully revoked 3700+ licenses. Initial design didn’t account for all of the ways that ITIL licensing was being used. Revisiting design and pausing automations until we have a more comprehensive solution.

  • Preparing for Gate 2 and scheduled gate 2 review (4/25/2022)

  • Combined ITOM Base and Project teams to consolidate skillsets to deliver 2021 Carryover items and continue major project deliverables.

  • Completed MSSQL Clusters, Solarwinds, Speed 2 Market (SCCM and OpenNMS) discovery items

  • Finalized Software Asset Management reclamation workflow and allowed us to realize estimated hard benefits of $5M savings in 2022 after 600 products are added to the workflow


March 2022:


  • Strategic retirement of BMC core foundation and infrastructure.

  • Proposed solution has received Architectural endorsement and adheres to Enterprise Mandates

  • Received initial HAM Pro Proof of Value using the inputs provided to Service Now and shared with technical stakeholders.

  • Created accelerated license automation plans to be executed if required. Currently working with procurement to determine when payment is due.

  • Finished MVP 1 of product catalog updates with plans to complete MVP 2 by April 30.
