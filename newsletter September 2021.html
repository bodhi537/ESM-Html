September 2021 Newsletter

“It’s the Final Countdown” – Europe Rock Band

FLCIT, UIT and Projects successfully migrated to ServiceNow this month. We now have 70% of BMC users on the new platform. The last organizations to onboard in mid-October are ITOE and CES.

We recognize this project does not mark the end of our ServiceNow journey. Next year we are planning to continue to expand on capabilities and encourage you to also build on the foundation delivered.

The End (really) is Near

As previously communicated, BMC will soon be retired and several changes will be happening October 31. Please ensure you are ready for this first milestone towards the retirement of BMC.

Software Asset Management

New capabilities have been recently introduced, including the ability to return or transfer an unused software, reserving software for someone else and introducing different device types (server software, SaaS and Information & Data Services)

New functionality coming late October includes:

  • Migration of 2k software products from BMC
  • Software packaging integrations (Apptrack, Flexera Admin Studio, ITCM, SMART Contract management)
  • Ability to request a software that is not published
  • More automated workflows

Last Call for Training

If you haven’t yet signed up to attend a live training class, please do so now as our last session will be held October 8th. Access goto/esmtraining to sign up.

Moving forward, you can access recorded trainings and associated materials.

Continuous Improvements

Incident Categorization

New fields are now available on the incident module to assist in categorizing incidents. Learn more here.

Approval Delegation

Want a simple way to delegate your ServiceNow approvals? Access the approvals page via the IT Service portal and select ‘Set alternate approver’.

Portal Improvements

We are continuing to enhance the new IT Service portal and have introduced advanced, AI search capabilities and enhanced My Approvals screen to name a few.
