ITOM Overview

Discovery offers a replicable and reliable method of identifying the enterprise IT Infrastructure. Discovery can find computers, servers, software, printers, routers, and switches. Basically, any IP-enabled devices and applications that run on them. This method is referred to as Horizontal discovery. The connections between teh devices and applications are not included in horizontal discovery.

Service Mapping provides a framework that connect between devices and applications which enables the top-down mapping approach to the discovered devices. This feature allows one to see impact of a problematic object on the service map.
