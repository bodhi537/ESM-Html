Our Mission

Drive maximum value delivery and innovation in Enterprise Service Management through common interface, tools, data and skills

  • The ESM Program is here to enhance service delivery, optimize business process and improve collaboration across the organization expanding service delivery for and beyond IT with our centralized service management platform-ServiceNow!
  • The world works with ServiceNow.

  • ESM's Service Centric Approach helps transform business processes by digitizing and automating customer needs to improve Service delivery experiences within the organization.
  • ESM establishes key performance indicators to measure performances of services rolled out.
  • This data driven approach helps align with customer needs and opens door for improvements.

  • ESM program helps standardize service delivery by promoting standardization and automation of routine business processes resulting in consistent service quality.
  • The program maintains strong customer focus by empowering service personalization based on needs.
  • We are here to create a culture of continuous improvements to service delivery enhancing customer experience.
