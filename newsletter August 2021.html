Recent Successes

IT Service Portal:

The new IT Service Portal (goto/it) went live August 18th. Please look for the banner at the top of the portal homepage that links to a ‘What’s New’ article. Further enhancements are coming in the next release, including additional search capabilities.

Organizations Live on ServiceNow:

Foundation Infrastructure, Applications SPE & TS and Corporate IT have successfully migrated to ServiceNow. Our next wave of organizations (FLCIT, UIT and Projects) is planned for September 14th. Please check out the full schedule here.

BMC Read-Only Coming Soon

BMC will be set to read-only October 29th. What does this mean to you?

  • All dependencies on BMC should be removed before October 29th
  • Changes can’t be made in BMC (e.g., Incidents, Changes, Work Orders)
  • TicketAPI will be set to read-only (and cannot be used to create/change objects
  • Teams no longer need to monitor two systems (eBonding will be retired)
