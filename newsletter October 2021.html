October 2021 Newsletter

Important BMC Read-Only Reminders

We are on track to turn BMC read-only October 31. See key milestones below:

October 31

  • Ticket API will be read-only until December 17
  • IT Console will be read-only until November 15
  • BMC ITSM Interface will be read-only until January 2022
  • BMC CMDB UI will be read-only
  • eBonding synchronization will be turned off

November 15

  • IT Console will be discontinued

December 17

  • Ticket API will be discontinued
  • BMC CMDB will be full read-only
  • ServiceNow becomes the authoritative CMDB

Read-Only Exceptions

We anticipate 95+% adherence with being ready for BMC going read-only October 31. However, if an exception needs to be considered, review this criteria to see if your use case may be eligible for an extension.

Ongoing Support

Our project may be ending but ongoing support will not. Our support organization will continue to have change migrations to Production every Friday! Please submit all tickets to the L1 ServiceNow Admin support queue.

Citizen Developers should continue participating in the weekly community meetings! Sign up here.

2022 ESM Expansion

Our ESM project may be closing out soon but not our continued to investment in the platform. We are planning to kickoff an ESM expansion project November 1st!

The detailed scope is being finalized but will include the following:

  • Full BMC Retirement
  • Citizen Developer Expansion (chat bots, mobile UI, etc.)
  • IT Operations Management Expansion
  • Software and Hardware Asset Management Enhancements
