ServiceNow Upgrade

ServiceNow releases two upgrades a year and we are nearing end of life of our current version at the end of this month.


  • We are upgrading from “Quebec” to “San Diego” version.
  • The upgrade process across all the environments will take about 40 days to complete and users will not be able to deploy any new functionality from April 15th until production is upgraded on May 28th.
  • Refer to goto/SNUpgrade for more details around the schedule, impact and action needed.

Do You know??

  • Ever heard of ATF – Automated Test Framework? You can now automate your testing in ServiceNow via ATF. Find more at Goto/ATFs and you can also join our CitizenDev CoP sessions in case of any questions.
  • Facing compatibility issues with ServiceNow forms while submitting any requests? Make sure to use Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome browser while using ServiceNow.
  • Which license would you need in ServiceNow? Check out Goto/SNLicense for more information on licensing and costs. If your ServiceNow needs don’t fit under any of these licenses, fill-in the survey at Goto/SNSurvey
  • Check out different roles within ServiceNow solution along with their responsibilities and decision rights at Goto/ServiceNowRnR
  • ITDW will be archiving all BMC historical data effective April 1st, 2022 and storing them in a newly created BMC_ARCHIVE DB on DALSQL850. All refreshes will be stopped and access to this data will be restricted. For more information regarding the BMC data archive and how to access it, you can visit Goto/ITDWArchivedBMCMarts or reach out to Roger Pierce/Luis Perez.

Analytics and Reporting

New capability for your dashboards :


Performance Analytics Widgets are now available to all dashboard creators in ServiceNow.

XOM IT Manager Dashboard Enhancement :


XOM IT Manager dashboard has been updated to include Requested Items and Catalog tasks. This dashboard is the one-stop place an IT Manager can use to review incidents, problems, requests, requested items, and catalog tasks that make up their team’s workload. You may access the dashboard within Service Now or by using the goto/ITManager.
