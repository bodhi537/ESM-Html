Solution Owner

Focused on offering a working solution that meets the needs of their customers and team.Their focus is on the business service and the experience of the service that they are offering.

This would normally align with a Product Manager or Product Owner level role

Responsibilities

  • Communication with and support of customers of their solution in case of any outages, service degradation, issues or changes to the solution.
  • Ability to maintain solution including having people/skills troubleshooting, fixing, developing, testing, and documenting their solutions within ServiceNow, including in preparation for platform upgrades.
  • Responsible for ensuring solution has a backup process through use of Code repository or alternative method.
  • Defining retention policies for any custom table data within your Solution per platform guardrails.
  • Responsible for owning and maintaining any tables and data created by your Solution.
  • Responsible for any cost or licenses associated with the Solution, such as custom table license.
  • Responsible for providing support routing information to ServiceNow Platform team so that customer issues can be sent to the appropriate support contacts.
  • Ensuring proper Solution Requests are submitted and approved by Domain Owner for any New Solutions or major enhancements to existing solutions.
  • Responsible for risk assessment of solutions they own on the ServiceNow Platform. ESMâ€™s ServiceNow Risk Assessment covers core platform risks, but does not cover solutions built on top of the platform that use external integrations or customized code/scripts.

Decision Rights

  • Approval of any new or modifications to Solution.
  • Decides how and who will support the solution.
