Important BMC Read-Only Reminders

We are on track to turn BMC read-only October 31. See key milestones below:

October 31

  • Ticket API will be read-only until December 17
  • IT Console will be read-only until November 15
  • BMC ITSM Interface will be read-only until January 2022
  • BMC CMDB UI will be read-only
  • eBonding synchronization will be turned off

November 15

  • IT Console will be discontinued

December 17

  • Ticket API will be discontinued
  • BMC CMDB will be full read-only
  • ServiceNow becomes the authoritative CMDB

We anticipate 95+% adherence with being ready for BMC going read-only October 31. However, if an exception needs to be considered, review this criteria to see if your use case may be eligible for an extension.

1Q 2021 Newsletter

What is ESM?

Enterprise Service Management (ESM) extends service management capabilities across the enterprise leveraging a common, integrated platform and portal. Building on a multi-year journey to optimize, consolidate and improve the overall service, ESM is now poised for the next transformative step in the process.

Our near-term project focus is to replace the end-of-live BMC platform and Aspera system. We have selected ServiceNow, a SaaS offering and industry-leading Service Management platform. In addition to its ‘out of the box’ core functionality and a modern, fully integration application, the investment in ServiceNow will provide a foundation for further expansion of integrated services across the enterprise.

- Establish an enterprise service management offering to improve visibility across the landscape

- Manage IT Service Demand through a common platform, portal and service catalog

- Provide foundation platform for future cross-process optimization

Check out our 2021 release schedule below:

- April: ITSM fundamentals and early Adopters, IT Software Asset Catalog

- July: Expanded Capabilities including more integrations

- September: End-to-end service management and advanced software asset capabilities

- December: BMC and Aspera become read-only/retired

We are excited to collaborate across EMIT as we take this next step towards more mature, consolidated service management processes. Stay tuned for more updates!
