About ESM

Who are we?


  • The Enterprise Service Management (ESM) Program provides the modern ServiceNow Platform that can enable both IT and business service management for the corporation.

  • The business functions that can benefit from proven service management best practices and capabilities such as ticketing, workflow and automation, knowledge management and self-service.

  • It improves operational efficiencies while maintaining a focus on customer or employee satisfaction.


Mission

  • Enterprise Service Management(ESM) extends service management to address service centric use cases and is a strategic opportunity for the corporation.

  • Drive maximum value, delivery and innovation in Enterprise Service Management through commom interface, tools, data and skills across the organization to help them fully utilize the tool's capabilities.

What do we do?

  • Enterprise Service Management enables enterprises to automate management workflows to boost efficiency and reduce errors.

  • We help in streamlining service delivery processess across multiple departments.

  • We centralize service requests and incident management, enhance communication and collaboration between teams and improve customer or employee satisfaction while optimizing costs.





How we do?

  • Create standard service delivery by promoting standardization and automation of routine business processes resulting in consistent service quality.

  • The program maintains strong customer focus by empowering service personalization based on needs.

  • We are here to create a culture of continuous improvements to service delivery enhancing customer experience.
